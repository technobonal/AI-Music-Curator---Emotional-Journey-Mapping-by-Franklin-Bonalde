## 🎯 Prompt Objective

To generate a 25-song auditory experience titled "**Sundays of Calm and Energy**". The design must follow an ascending energy curve, seamlessly integrating organic and electronic genres.

## 📝 The Prompt:

"Create a playlist titled **“Sundays of Calm and Energy”** focused on an emotional and sonic journey that accompanies a perfect Sunday, from morning until night.

### Constraints and Parameters:

1. **Length:** Approximately 25 songs.
2. **Structure:** Divide the list into 5 thematic blocks (5 songs per block) following an emotional and energetic progression.
3. **Energy Curve:** Start with soft/relaxed sounds (Morning) and end with modern/danceable rhythms (Night).
4. **Allowed Genres:**
    - Melodic Techno / House
    - Deep House
    - Downtempo / Ambient
    - Latin Jazz
    - Organic sounds (real instrumentation)

### Expected Output Format:
Provide a table with the following columns: **Thematic Block | Artist | Song | Genre | Time of Day**."
